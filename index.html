Our Story

A Palace Born for Celebrations

In 1995, Maharaj Prithviraj Singh transformed his ancestral haveli in Jaipur into Shaadi Mahal — a sanctuary where the grandeur of Indian wedding traditions could be preserved and celebrated in all their glory.

What began as a modest heritage property has blossomed into Rajasthan's most coveted wedding destination, welcoming families from across the world who seek an authentic yet luxurious celebration of love.

Today, under the stewardship of the third generation, Shaadi Mahal continues to honour every ceremony with the same devotion and attention to detail that has made us a legend in luxury Indian weddings.
